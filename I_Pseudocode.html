Insertion Sort

Pseudocode


			Algoritma INSERTION-SORT(A)
			1: for j = 2 to A:length do
			2: 	 key = A[j]
			3: 	 i = j - 1
			4:	 while i > 0 and A[i] > key do
			5: 		A[i + 1] = A[i]
			6:		i = i - 1
			7: 	 end while
			8:   A[i + 1] = key
			9: end for
